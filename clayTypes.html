Clay Types and Pricing

All of our clays are sold in 25lb bags and can be purchased in the studio.

B-Mix (no grog) $20

B-Mix has a smooth, porcelain texture, and fires to a cream color in oxidation.

B-Mix w/speckles $22

Look...it's B-Mix...but with speckles!

Weststone $20

Weststone has a slightly coarse texture and fires to yellow/brown in oxidation.

Speckled Buff $20

Speckled buff is smooth textured and fires into the tan/brown color range.

Frost $33

Frost is an exceptionally white and translucent throwing porcelain for Cone 5-6.

Redstone $20

A red stoneware clay body with medium grog and minimal porosity at Cone 6. Fires to a warm brick red color in oxidation.

Moroccan Sand $22

Light beige fired in oxidation, the body is quite vitreous, ideal for throwing functional ware.

Greystone $22

This medium to smooth textured clay fires gray in oxidation.
